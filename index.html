A letter, just for you

My dearest Iris, I know this past week didn't feel like much. Maybe it felt like the days blurred together, like you were just going through the motions, like nothing exciting happened and nothing particularly good came your way. I know that feeling, and I want you to know — it's okay. Not every week needs to sparkle.

But here's what I also know: even in your quietest, most unremarkable weeks, you are still the most remarkable person I've ever met. The way you carry yourself. The little things you care about. The sound of your voice. None of that dimmed this week. Not even a little.

I built this page because I wanted you to have somewhere to come when the world feels grey — a small, warm corner that belongs entirely to you. A place that says: you are seen, you are loved, you are enough.

Next week might be better. It might be just as quiet. Either way, I'll be here — your biggest fan, your softest place to land.

Always,
💌 yours
