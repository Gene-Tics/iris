8 months of choosing you

it’s been 8 months… not perfect… not always easy… but always real.
i didn’t stay because things were simple… i stayed because you mattered to me.
i learned your quiet… your distance… and instead of leaving, i stayed softer.
i don’t love you loudly… i love you in ways that stay… even in silence.
why i choose you

i choose you in the moments you pull away… i choose you when things feel uncertain… i choose you even when it would be easier not to.
♥

i’m still here… and i’m still choosing you.

8 months later… and i never once felt like leaving.

not because everything was perfect… but because you were worth staying for.

and if you ever wonder if i’ll get tired… just know… i already chose you on the days it wasn’t easy.
