8 months of choosing you

it’s been 8 months… and i’m still here.
not because it was always easy… but because it was always you.
i know you can be distant sometimes… quiet… hard to read… but i never saw that as a reason to leave.
i saw it as something worth understanding.
i didn’t choose you once… i chose you on random days… on confusing days… on quiet days.
and somehow… i never got tired of it.
♥

you mean more to me than i usually say.

and i notice more about you than you think.

i’m still choosing you… quietly… but sincerely.
these past 8 months… weren’t just time passing for me.

they were me learning you… understanding you… staying even when things felt uncertain.

i won’t say everything was perfect… because it wasn’t… but what i felt for you stayed real the whole time.

you became someone i think about in small moments… in random thoughts… in quiet times.

and i don’t need everything to be rushed… i just need it to be genuine.

and with you… it always has been.
